Route Mapping Assistant

Optimise your routes effortlessly with the Mapping Assistant feature of buddi. Avoid obstacles such as low bridges and narrow roads, ensuring smooth navigation from stop to stop. Whether you prefer to create your routes manually or have them automatically generated, buddi offers the flexibility to suit your preferences.

Once created, these routes can be seamlessly integrated into the driver's app, serving as a comprehensive route guide. This not only provides convenience but also instills confidence in both you and the driver, that the designated route will be followed accurately. With each stop and destination clearly mapped out, you can rest assured that every journey is executed safely and efficiently, enhancing the experience for both drivers and passengers.
