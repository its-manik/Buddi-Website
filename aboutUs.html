About Us

Originating from the largest coach travel company in the East Midlands, Roberts Travel Group, buddi stemmed from a small idea focused on school transport management. It aimed to assist staff members in simplifying application processes, managing bus capacity, and optimising loadings.

From this concept, buddi was born. Developed by an operator that is intimately familiar with the intricacies of running an effective and reliable organisation, buddi is now ready and here to help.

Created by operators, for operators.

How we help

Suitable for both large corporations and small enterprises, as well as schools, local authorities, and many other entities, buddi is here to lend a helping hand with all transportation needs.

Whether it's optimising fleet management, ensuring the safety and security of passengers, or streamlining logistical operations, buddi offers a versatile and efficient solution that can be tailored to fit the unique requirements of any organisation.

With cutting-edge technology and user-friendly interface, buddi empowers businesses to enhance productivity, reduce costs, and provide an unparalleled level of service to customers and stakeholders.
